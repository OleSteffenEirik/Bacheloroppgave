Tronrud Engineering

At Tronrud Engineering, we have always been at the forefront of "Innovation” in manufacturing. Over the past 40 years, we have developed, produced and delivered customized industrial products and technical solutions for our Customers –
bringing them competitive advantage and profitability. The company currently has around 118 employees at its premises in Eggemoen, plus 38 in its two subsidiaries – one in Singapore and one in Moss, south of Oslo.
